
In Nifty or Indian Index major sector is services and Finacials which is similar to the index choosen by me IBEX-35 as it also have majors in financials followed by energy as seen in indian indices.


Why not other two?
    - OMXS: has export major sector as dominnat
    - ATX: too small to be compared

Indian Indices: 
    - Financials (Banks & NBFCs)
    - Energy & Utilities
    - Large domestic consumption
    - Moderate export exposure (IT, pharma)

IBEX-35:
    - Financials (Banks)
    - Utilities & Energy
    - Infrastructure-heavy
    - Largely domestic + regional (EU/LATAM) exposure

OMXS-30:
    - Industrials & Manufacturing
    - Export-oriented global companies
    - Strong exposure to global trade cycles
    - High sensitivity to USD/EUR exchange rates
    - Technology-enabled industrials, automation, engineering

ATX-20:
    - Financials (highly concentrated in banks)
    - Energy & Utilities (oil, gas, power)
    - Significant exposure to Central & Eastern Europe
    - Smaller index with few dominant stocks


So both IBEX-35 and ATX-20 resonates with Indian indices but we will go for IBEX-35 as it has comparable volume wrt Indian indices.
